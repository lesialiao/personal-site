Travel Bites

Antelope Canyon, Page, AZ, USA, Canon EOS Rebel T3, December 26, 2014

Traveling to eat or eating to travel?

I've had numerous chances to travel during my twenty years of life. Whether it be road-tripping around the cities of the East Coast or trekking the landscapes of the world, I'm grateful for these opportunities to explore new places, new cultures, and of course, new foods! However, I've had hard times documenting these trips and stories aside from massive photo albums on my hard drive that may eventually make it through the pipeline of sorting, editing, printing, and publishing to social media.

This fall, I'm studying abroad at Yale-NUS College, Singapore, and I hope to write and blog about my adventures here. Follow my journey here
